About

On August 2nd, I'll ride the 1-day PMC route from Wellesley to Bourne, MA, joined by almost 7,000 other riders.

I am grateful to honor my dad's journey by riding the PMC, alongside my mom.

The Pan-Mass Challenge has raised over $1 BILLION for the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute, with 100% of every rider-raised dollar going directly to fund cancer research. Thanks to DFCI, my dad's cancer prognosis was extended from months to years, enabling him to ride in 2011.

Considering those who battled cancer or are still striving for a healthy outcome, let's make a difference.

Please join me in my personal commitment to raise $4,000 this year, supporting cancer patients and their families. Your generosity will contribute to funding research for a cure and developing a supportive community.

I would be proud to ride in honor of or in memory of your loved one affected by cancer.

PMC donations are tax deductible and 100% will go to Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. The PMC's tax ID / EIN is 04-2746912.
