About

I've committed to raising $4,000.00 for cancer research by riding in the 2025 Pan-Mass Challenge. On the first weekend in August, I will join thousands of cyclists in the PMC, an annual bike-a-thon that raises money for research and care at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute (DFCI) in Boston.

I hope I can count on your support

The PMC raises more money for charity than any other single event in the country - more than $1 billion since 1980 and a record $75 million in 2024 alone! This success is the result of a lot of people riding for and caring about a cure. And because every dollar matters, 100 percent of your donation goes to Dana-Farber.

I've made a personal commitment to ride and raise $4,000.00, so I hope you can help me achieve this significant goal, which is part of the PMC's 2025 fundraising.

PMC donations are tax deductible and 100% will go to Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. The PMC's tax ID / EIN is 04-2746912.
